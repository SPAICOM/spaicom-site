Lucia Testa is a postdoctoral fellow at UKE in the IMSB group, supervised by Prof. Stefan Bonn. She earned an M.Sc. in Data Science from Sapienza University of Rome, graduating summa cum laude and ranking among the top 1.5% of students, under the supervision of Prof. Simone Scardapane. She also received a Ph.D. in Data Science from Sapienza University of Rome, summa cum laude, supervised by Prof. Sergio Barbarossa. Her research focuses on the theory and methods of topological signal processing and deep learning, with applications in drug discovery and omics data, including scRNA-seq and spatial transcriptomics. She was awarded the Prize for the Best Engineering M.Sc. Thesis (2020/2021) by Fondazione Sapienza.
