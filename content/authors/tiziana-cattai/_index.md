Tiziana Cattai is assistant professor at DIET, Sapienza University of Rome since 2023. She received her MSc in biomedical engineering in Sapienza (2017) and her PhD in cotutelle between Sorbonne Universite (Ecole Doctorale Informatique, Telecommunications et Electronique) and Sapienza University of Rome (2021). She received a postdoctoral fellowship at the Paris Brain Institute (April -September 2021) in collaboration with IBM and postdoctoral fellowships (2022-2023) at DIET, Sapienza University.
She has co-authored 16 journal papers and several conference papers. Her research interests include signal processing and learning with applications to real systems, such as smart water grids, brain and wireless networks. She won the best paper award in ALGOCLOUD conference in 2025.
Her teaching activity includes the course "Projet Informatique" at Sorbonne Université (2019), "Fundamentals of Communications" (2023-24,2024-25, BSc in Computer Engineering, Sapienza), "Probability and Statistics" (Sapienza master, 2022). For WiNK company, she taught “Internet of things" and "Data Visualization" (2021).  She held invited seminars at EPFL, Lausanne (2021) and at CNAM, Paris (2023). 
She co-chaired three editions of the IFIP SmartWater workshop (2024, 2025, 2026) and is/was Publication (EUVIP 2024), Student Session (EUVIP 2023, 2025), and Workshop (IFIP 2024) chair. She served as reviewer for many journals (such as IEEE TSIPN, TMM, TNN, TNSRE).
